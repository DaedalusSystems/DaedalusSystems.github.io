Who this is for

You don’t need “more software”. You need your existing tools to work harder.

Daedalus Systems is for agents who already have a CRM, a website, and an inbox full of clients—and want those tools to quietly do more. Instead of dragging you into a new platform, we wire Talos into what’s already on your desk.

  • You want consistent follow-up, but you’re often in the field when new leads arrive.
  • You’ve tried complex systems before and don’t want another dashboard to babysit.
  • You care about your voice and don’t want an AI pretending to be you without guardrails.

What it feels like to work with Talos

The goal isn’t to remove you from your business—it’s to protect the parts that need a human. Talos simply picks up the repetitive labor: the first reply, the intake questions, the data entry.

Your mornings

You open your CRM to a short, clear list: who reached out, what they want, and the best next step. No hunting through portals, no tangled email threads.

Your showings

While you’re in a listing appointment or driving between properties, Talos answers new inquiries in your voice—so you’re not racing to text from the driveway.

Your evenings

Instead of catching up on “I’ll reply later” messages, you review a tidy set of summaries and decide where your energy is best spent.
