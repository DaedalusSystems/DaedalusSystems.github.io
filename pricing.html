Starter Package – Single Workflow

Best for: trying automation on one high-impact process.

A focused build to prove the value of automation in your day-to-day work, without overhauling your entire system.

  • One well-defined workflow (e.g. new lead → CRM + instant response).
  • Discovery and mapping session to understand your current process.
  • Build, test, and launch of the automation.
  • Light documentation and a live walkthrough so you can own it.

Ideal if you want to see real results quickly and understand how a full system could work for you later.

Core System – Digital Front Desk

Best for: agents and small teams ready to automate their whole intake.

Your lead handling, follow-up, and basic admin are turned into a cohesive system that runs in the background while you focus on clients.

  • Multiple connected workflows (lead capture, follow-up, CRM sync, reminders).
  • Custom rules for routing, tagging, and prioritizing new inquiries.
  • Launch support and a refinement period to tune the automations to reality.
  • Option to add an ongoing maintenance retainer for tweaks and new ideas.

This is the package for building a true “automated front desk” that feels like part of your business, not just a bolt-on tool.

Custom Build

For more complex operations or non-real-estate businesses, we scope a custom project around your exact tools, data, and requirements.

  • Workflow mapping across multiple systems and teams, if needed.
  • Automations designed to fit your existing stack, not replace it.
  • Room for specialized logic, approvals, or reporting.

If you’re not sure where you fit, we can use a short call to map your processes and recommend the right level of build.
