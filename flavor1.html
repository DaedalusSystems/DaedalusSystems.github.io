Orientation

We start with your current reality, not a blank canvas.

Before we automate anything, we map the tools and habits you already rely on: email, phone, portals, website forms, and the way you actually track clients. The goal is simple—make your existing workflow feel lighter, not introduce a new one.

  • Lead sources: where do new conversations originate today?
  • Your voice: how do you greet, qualify, and follow up?
  • Your CRM: which fields, tags, and stages actually matter?
  • Your week: when are you most stretched, and what slips?
Talos in practice

A tireless, polite first pass on every lead.

Talos listens for new inquiries, responds in seconds, and gently gathers the details you need. Every conversation is stored where you expect it—in your CRM—and every morning you see a clear list of who actually needs your call.

You choose how far we go:

  • Simple mode: instant acknowledgment, basic info capture.
  • Standard mode: smarter questions, soft qualification.
  • Atelier mode: layered routing, team handoffs, niche workflows.
