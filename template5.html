What actually happens when a lead comes in

Watch Talos turn a cold inquiry into a clear next step.

This is the end-to-end journey for a buyer who fills out a form on your site at 11:47 PM while you’re offline.

Step 01
The inquiry arrives

A new lead submits your existing contact form or portal lead. Talos listens in the background — no change to your website, no new login.

0–2 seconds
Step 02
Talos replies in your voice

An on-brand message goes out automatically, acknowledging their request, setting expectations, and asking one or two clarifying questions.

Script is handcrafted during onboarding, then tuned over time.
Step 03
Details are captured

As the lead responds, Talos quietly collects key facts: timing, price range, location, and whether they’re already working with another agent.

You’ll see the full conversation inside your CRM record.
Step 04
Your CRM is updated

The contact is created (or updated) in your existing system with tags, notes, and tasks that match your workflow — Follow Up Boss, Wise Agent, LionDesk, and more.

Routing logic is custom to your pipeline stages and tags.
Step 05
You get a focused alert

Instead of a generic email, you receive a concise summary and a recommended next action: call, text, or schedule — with context Talos has already gathered.

You step in only when a human voice is needed.

This demo flow is tuned for a solo Boulder agent. In your build, we adjust timing, language, and routing rules to mirror how you already work.
